# FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certification Course
## Comprehensive College-Level Curriculum with Official FAA Content

---

## Course Overview

This comprehensive FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certification course is designed to prepare students for the FAA aeronautical knowledge test required for commercial drone operations. The course integrates official FAA regulations, study guides, and government resources into a structured college-level curriculum.

### Course Details
- **Duration**: 40 hours of instruction
- **Modules**: 15 comprehensive modules
- **Level**: Intermediate (College-level)
- **Price**: $499.00
- **Certification**: Prepares students for FAA Part 107 Knowledge Test

## Certification Path

### After Completing This Course:

1. **Schedule Your FAA Knowledge Test**
   - Find a testing center: https://faa.psiexams.com/faa/login
   - Test fee: Approximately $175 (subject to change)
   - Bring government-issued photo ID

2. **Pass the Knowledge Test**
   - 60 questions, 2-hour time limit
   - Minimum 70% passing score
   - Receive Airman Knowledge Test Report

3. **Complete FAA Form 8710-13**
   - Apply via FAA DroneZone: https://faadronezone.faa.gov/
   - Include knowledge test results
   - Complete TSA vetting

4. **Receive Your Remote Pilot Certificate**
   - Temporary certificate available immediately
   - Permanent certificate mailed within weeks
   - Valid indefinitely (recurrent training required every 24 months)

5. **Register Your sUAS**
   - Register at https://faadronezone.faa.gov/
   - Cost: $5 for 3 years
   - Display registration number on aircraft

6. **Begin Commercial Operations!**
   - Carry certificate during all operations
   - Follow all Part 107 regulations
   - Maintain recurrent training currency
